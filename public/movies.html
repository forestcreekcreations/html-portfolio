The Best Movies According to Dan

My top 3 Movies of all time

Donnie Darko

This movie has so many good elements that make you think, and it really messes with your senses.
Plus I love Frank.

Deadpool

Deadpool has always been my favourite character, and the fact that they made 3 movies for him just tickles me.

Fight Club

Fight Club has always held a special place in my heart. I love the interactions between Brad Pitt and Ed Norton, they play well off of each other, and the chemistry between them and Helena Bonham Carter is fantastic.
