About Me

I'm a hands-on problem solver with over 20 years of real-world experience across industries-construction, oilfield, hospitality, sales, and now digital design. I've worn a lot of hats over the years: from running a restaurant shift solo at 15 while homeschooling, to managing renovation sites, selling funeral pre-planning services, and spending long days on drilling rigs. Every one of those roles has sharpened my instincts for hard work, communication, and adapting on the fly.


Today, I'm channeling that same grit and independence into building websites and digital tools-especially for local tradespeople and small business owners who want something solid and authentic online. I know what it means to work with your hands, talk straight, and deliver real value, because I've lived it.


Whether I'm designing a clean, mobile-friendly site or helping someone rebrand their business, I bring the same work ethic and creative energy I've carried through every job I've ever done.
